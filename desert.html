The Desert

What is an desert?

A desert is a very dry place.

In the desert there is only a small amount of rain.

What animals live in the desert?

  • Camels
  • Rattlesnakes
  • Scorpions
  • Iguanas
  • Tortoises

What plants live in the desert?

  • Cactus
  • Palm Trees
  • Tumbleweed
  • California Poppy
